About IndabaX Rwanda

IndabaX Rwanda is a 2-day AI and Machine Learning conference that aims to build the AI and ML community in Rwanda. It is a local version of the Deep Learning Indaba aiming to connect the local community with the wider African AI community and to bring international expertise and speakers to the community.

The mission of IndabaX Rwanda is multifaceted: to elevate AI and machine learning capabilities across Rwanda, to showcase Rwanda as a beacon of innovation in the AI landscape, and to attract top talent and investment to the region. By hosting this event in Kigali, IndabaX Rwanda aims to provide a platform for learning, networking, and collaboration that will fuel the growth and impact of AI in Africa.
